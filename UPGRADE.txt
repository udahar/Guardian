8. The only real weaknesses

Three things I’d improve.

1. Event bus

Right now modules call each other directly.

Better model:

event_bus.publish("cpu.high")
guardian.subscribe("cpu.high")

You already hinted at event bus architecture earlier.

2. decision engine

Currently rules are scattered.

You want a central:

decision_engine.py

Where Guardian decides:

monitor
warn
heal
kill
ignore
3. metrics compression

Guardian will generate a lot of metrics.

You’ll eventually need:

aggregation windows
sampling
deduplication

Otherwise Postgres grows fast.
